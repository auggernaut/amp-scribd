Nouns are naming words. Nouns are names of people, places, animals and
things.

Places: India, school, garden, park.
Animals: lion, fox, cat, monkey.

Nouns that stand for only one person, place, animal or thing are said
to be singular.
Nouns that stand for more than one person, place, animal or thing
are said to be plural.
To make plural of a noun we add ‘s’
Example:
tree
trees

All sentences begin with a capital letter and end with a full stop.
Names of people and places begin with a capital letter.
The word I is always written as a capital letter.
Example:
I like to play games.
Ali is a good boy.
I am from India.
